Our Story

From Cuba to San Diego

Sandra Cardet's journey began in Cuba, where she learned the art of cooking from her mother and grandmother. At age 12, she moved to San Diego, bringing with her generations of authentic family recipes and a passion for her cultural heritage.

Growing up in Pacific Beach and attending Mission Bay High School, Sandra never forgot the flavors of home. Her Cuban and Syrian heritage merged into a unique culinary perspective that would one day become Havana Grill.

A New Chapter

Before opening Havana Grill, Sandra had a remarkable career as a pioneering woman stockbroker on Wall Street and later ran her own construction company. But it was her son Alexander who recognized her true calling.

Inspired by a movie and his mother's incredible cooking, Alexander encouraged Sandra to share her passion with the world. In 2016, with no prior restaurant experience but decades of love and expertise, Havana Grill was born.

Quality & Heart

At Havana Grill, we reimagine traditional Cuban comfort food with a modern, healthy approach. We use only the finest organic greens, hormone-free meats, and non-GMO cooking oils.

Every dish tells a story. Every ingredient is carefully chosen. Every meal is prepared with the same love and care that Sandra's grandmother brought to her kitchen in Cuba.

Growing Together

Today, Havana Grill thrives in two San Diego locations: Clairemont Mesa and Mission Valley. Our success is built on community, authenticity, and the unwavering belief that great food brings people together.

We're proud to support our community through inclusive hiring practices and internship opportunities, proving that a restaurant can be both profitable and purposeful.
